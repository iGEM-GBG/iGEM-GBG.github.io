Welcome to iGEM-cga

We are the iGEM Chalmers Gothenburg association. We were founded in 2023 with the aim of supporting the Chalmers iGEM team during the iGEM competition and to create a continuity between teams.

iGEM is the world's biggest competition in synthetic biology, where multidisciplinary teams from all over the world tackle everyday issues facing the world.

On this website you can find information about the iGEM competition, previous and current projects, about how to join the next year's iGEM Chalmers team and more!
