ALUMNI DATA TEMPLATE
====================

Fill out this template for each alumni and save as alumni-NAME.txt (e.g., alumni-john-doe.txt)

REQUIRED FIELDS (must be filled):
ALUMNI NAME: John Doe

OPTIONAL FIELDS (can be omitted if not applicable):
ALUMNI IMAGE: alumni-john-doe.jpg
ALUMNI YEAR: 2022
ALUMNI POSITION: Team Lead
ALUMNI STORY: What iGEM meant to me and my career. This is where alumni write about their experience...
You can write multiple lines in the story. Just continue on the next line.
Double line breaks create new paragraphs. Single line breaks create line breaks within paragraphs.
ALUMNI IMAGE OFFSET: center
ALUMNI IMAGE ZOOM: medium

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Copy this template for each alumni
2. Fill in the actual information
3. Save as alumni-NAME.txt (replace NAME with their name, use hyphens for spaces)
4. Place alumni images in the images/ folder (portrait format recommended)
5. Run the update script to regenerate the alumni stories page

EXAMPLE WITH ALL FIELDS:
ALUMNI NAME: Sarah Johnson
ALUMNI IMAGE: alumni-sarah-johnson.jpg
ALUMNI YEAR: 2022
ALUMNI POSITION: Team Lead
ALUMNI STORY: Participating in iGEM was a transformative experience that shaped my career path. The competition taught me valuable skills in synthetic biology, project management, and teamwork. It opened doors to opportunities I never imagined and connected me with a global community of passionate scientists.

EXAMPLE WITH MINIMAL FIELDS (only name):
ALUMNI NAME: Alex Chen
ALUMNI IMAGE: alumni-alex-chen.jpg

EXAMPLE WITH STORY BUT NO YEAR/POSITION:
ALUMNI NAME: Maria Rodriguez
ALUMNI IMAGE: alumni-maria-rodriguez.jpg
ALUMNI STORY: iGEM was one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my academic career. The hands-on laboratory work and the opportunity to present at the Giant Jamboree gave me confidence in my abilities and helped me discover my passion for research.

EXAMPLE WITH MULTI-LINE STORY (using line breaks):
ALUMNI NAME: John Smith
ALUMNI IMAGE: alumni-john-smith.jpg
ALUMNI YEAR: 2020
ALUMNI STORY: Participating in iGEM was a transformative experience that shaped my career path.
The competition taught me valuable skills in synthetic biology, project management, and teamwork.

It opened doors to opportunities I never imagined and connected me with a global community of passionate scientists. Today, I work as a research scientist at a biotech company, and I still use the skills I learned in iGEM every day.

IMAGE ADJUSTMENT OPTIONS:
Use ALUMNI IMAGE OFFSET to fix cropping (e.g., missing forehead):
- top, center, bottom (predefined)
- custom:10% or custom:-20px (custom positioning)

Use ALUMNI IMAGE ZOOM to adjust size (make people similar sizes):
- small, medium, large (predefined)
- custom:0.9 or custom:1.2 (custom scale)
